Nicely seasoned burgers

There is nothing like a good american burger. Often times I feel like alot of people who eat burgers are bias in regards of quality and taste of the beef and are blinded by the condiments and toppings that are included or requested. To be honest though I'm so in love with burgers times I will settle for a below average burger just to fulfill my craving lol smh.

Taco's, Quesadilla's', Tamale's, Enchalada's, etc

I have always been in love with hispanic cuisine. One of the main reasons is because of the variety that comes with eating hispanic food. I can easily say that I love street tacos the most but the next day, I'll go to a friends house and get blow away by the enchaladas that was made. I get the slightest feeling that there is more out there than what I've had so far. Sounds like its time to experiment!!

From Pho' to Thai to Teriyaki

I feel, well at least for me, living in Seattle we have tons to choose from when it comes to asian food. Especially for me growing up with alot of my friends being Phillpino or Cambodian etc. I have had the opportunity to try so many different types of asian food, or I could also say food from the pacific island. All I do know is, growing up with the friends I have makes me want to travel throughout Asia and just eat allll day!!

Pizza, Pizza, Pizza

Pizza, pizza and more pizza! I first want to shout out the italian person who decided to change the game up and introduce this wonderful pie full of flavor. Mmmhhmmm I would also like to shout out Domino's and Pizza Hut pizza for being in competition for who's has the best carryout deals on large 3 toppings lol. Lastly I would like to give a final shout out to Big Marios pizza in Cap Hill for always provided my stomach with the deliciously regretful calories on those drunk late nights! Much appreciated!! =]

Sandwiches galore!!

I know they're probably the simplest entree I've talked about so far, but they have played a vital part in my life thus far! From starting out only eating Subway to eating Quizno's to going to QFC for a sandwich on my lunch to making them at my house to not spend money. I've always loved a good sandwich. The main thing I love about sandwiches are you can dress them however you like!! At QFC the employees would make fun of me because I would put turkey tuna and chicken on my sandwich lol. I don't care though I think its dish delish! In my opinion thats makes it all the worth wild.

They say it's the most important meal!

I honestly this is probably the only thing I look forward to before going to sleep. As well as helping me wake up early in the morning so I don't end up missing it. I just recently tried out egg benedict and I fell in love with it so much that I started making it myself! Thanks to chef Ramsey and my love for flavor I think my benedict is competing with anybody.

Sushi has slowly grown on me =]

I used to always think sushi was disgusting back then. In my head I'm thinking why would want to eat something thats undercooked. I was thinking wouldn't this increase the mercury intake(of course no). Anyways I went with a friend who said she loves it and I went through the menu and fortunately I found some fried items and they were amazing! I mean I guess this could mean that maybe I'm still not a fan of sushi other than the friend items ha. I'm not sure but hopefully one day I'll try something actually raw.

There's no food like soul food!

I feel like you just need to be lucky and know somebody or know the spots to go to, to get some good southern cuisine. Especially in Seattle where the options and places are scattered and not easily publicized or marketed. If you happen to find a place, thumbs up to you because the food is absolutely amazing if it is made correctly and made with love. If I had someone to cook soul food for me daily I would definitely be a "Big Boy" lol. It's definitely heavy food you probably could eat one setting of it and your done for the day!
